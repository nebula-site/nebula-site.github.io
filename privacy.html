Privacy Policy for Nebula


This Privacy Policy explains how Nebula (the "Site") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit / or use the Nebula Chat service. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.


1. Information We Collect


Chat & Profile Data: When you use the chat feature, we collect and store the following in our database (Supabase):

  • Messages: The text content of your chat messages.
  • Profile Info: Your chosen username and avatar URL.
  • Metadata: Timestamps of when messages were sent.

Note: Chat messages are public to all users of the Nebula network. Do not share private or sensitive information in the chat.


Personal Information via Forms: When you submit contact forms, bug reports, or suggestions, you may provide your name and email. This data is handled by third-party services (Formspree and Supabase).


Local Data: We use localStorage to store your profile settings (nebula_profile) and site preferences (favorites, view counts). This stays on your device and is used to identify you when you post messages.


2. How We Use Information

We use your data to facilitate the live chat experience, display your identity alongside your messages, respond to your inquiries, and improve site features. Your profile information is used to "autofill" your identity in the chat interface.


3. Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, your chat messages and profile display name are shared publicly with other visitors to the site. We use the following third-party processors:

  • Supabase: For database storage and real-time message delivery.
  • Formspree: For processing contact form submissions.

4. Your Choices and Rights

You can clear your localStorage at any time through your browser settings to remove your profile from your device. Since chat messages are stored persistently in our database, if you wish to have a specific message deleted, please contact us via the Contact page.


5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, because chat messages are designed to be viewed by other users, the "Security" of chat content is limited to its public visibility. Please use the network responsibly.


6. Data Retention

Chat messages are retained indefinitely to provide a continuous conversation history for the community. We may periodically clear older messages to maintain system performance.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please use the Contact page.
