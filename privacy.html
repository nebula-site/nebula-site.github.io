Privacy Policy for Nebula


This Privacy Policy explains how Nebula (the "Site") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit / or other pages on this site. The Site is a static website that offers games, content pages, and forms for contacting the site owner. By using the Site you agree to the practices described here.


What information we collect


Personal information you provide: When you submit the contact form (or other forms such as bug reports, suggestions or reviews), you may provide personal information such as your name, email address, and the message content. The contact form on this site is handled by a third-party form service (Formspree). When you submit the form, that data is transmitted to and stored by Formspree. Please review Formspree's privacy policy for details about how they process form submissions.


Local data stored in your browser: The Site uses browser storage (localStorage and sessionStorage) for client-side features only. Examples include:

  • Profile information saved locally (key: nebula_profile) to autofill the contact form and personalize the UI;
  • Favorites, view counts, and short-lived session values used to navigate between pages (stored in localStorage or sessionStorage).
These items are stored only on your device and are not sent to the Site's server by the site itself unless you submit them intentionally (for example, by submitting the contact form).


Automated / non-personal information: The Site does not currently use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or ad networks. We do not collect aggregated pageview analytics on the server side. If this changes we will update this policy.


How we use information

We use information you provide to respond to messages, process requests (e.g., bug reports or game suggestions), and to operate and improve the Site. Local browser storage is used to improve your experience (remembering favorites, profile info for autofill, and short-term session state).


Sharing and third parties

We do not sell personal data. Information you submit via the contact form is transmitted to a third-party form processor (Formspree). Aside from that service and standard content delivery (images, scripts loaded from this repository), we do not share personal information with other third parties. Third-party services used by the Site may have their own privacy policies and may set cookies or store data; please consult their policies directly.


Cookies and similar technologies

The Site itself does not set tracking cookies. Browser storage (localStorage and sessionStorage) is used for features described above. Third parties (for example, the form processor) may set cookies or use tracking; check their privacy pages for details.

Data retention
